— FAQs

Questions? Look here.

What services do you offer for VTubers?

I offer a wide range of services including logo design, stream overlays, character sheets, debut slides, and promotional assets specifically tailored for VTubers and content creators.

Can I get a custom package instead of individual items?

Absolutely! You can mix and match the services you need. We can discuss your requirements and I'll create a customized bundle tailored specifically to your channel's branding.

What is the typical turnaround time for a project?

The timeline depends on the complexity of the project. A standard stream overlay package usually takes 1-2 weeks, while a full branding suite might take up to 4 weeks. I will provide a clear timeline during our initial consultation.

What tools do you use for your design work?

My primary tools are Adobe Illustrator for vector work (like logos), Photoshop for raster and texturing, and After Effects for animated stream elements (stinger transitions, animated screens).
